What is acne?

Acne is a common skin condition where the pores of your skin clog.You'll usually get pimples on your face.Pores blockages produce blackheads,whiteheads and any other types of pimple.Pimples are pus-filled,sometimes painful bumps on your skin.

The medical term for acne is acne vulgaris.

OVERVIEW

Acne

The most common places where you might have acne are on your:

  • Face
  • Forehead
  • Chest
  • Shoulders
  • Upper back
Oil glands exist all over your body.The common locations of acne are where oil glands exists most.

SYMPTOMS AND CAUSES

Symptoms of acne on your skin include:

  • Pimples(pustules):Pus-filled bumps(papules)
  • Blackheads:Plugged pores with a black top
  • Whiteheads:Plugged pores with a white top
  • Cyst:Painful fluid-filled(pus)lumps under your skin

Clogged hair follicles or pores cause acne.Your hair follicles are small tubes that hold a strand of your hair

Acne might occur,for example,when:
  • your follicles produce too much oil
  • bacteria build up in your pores
  • dead skin cells accumulate in your pores

PROTECTION

  • Wash face twice daily
  • Avoid contact with greasy and oily substances
  • Protect skin from sun by applying moisturizers and sunscreens
  • Avoid friction and pressure on the skin
  • Do not touch, prick, or scratch the affected area
  • Shower after strenuous activities
  • Get adequate rest and sleep
